Why Netherlands? Why HZ? Why ICT?

To be honest I am not sure why HZ, Netherlands. Probably because I have heard a lot of good things
from my friends who study in the Netherlands and from what I saw I really liked it.

The people, the cities, the country, so many opportunities, all of it sounded very attractive.


The study programme suits my personality because ...

Since I was young I had interests in everything new to me, especially technology and computers were not an exception. To be honest, at first I showed the most interest in video games and to this day from time to time I enjoy them. At the beggining,
I had no idea how people made computers, programms, I just enjoyed using them. But after some time I started wondering how I could make one myself, manage it and most importantly make peoples life easier. Since then it took my attention.

Study program provided by HZ looked very interesting and different from what I saw back in my country. You get a lot of practise with real people, companies, you can choose the path you want to go while studing. Most importantly you have a idea
of what you want to do after you finish your study.
